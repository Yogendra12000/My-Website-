🎮 Types of Games for Beginners

🎮 Action Games

These involve fast-paced gameplay, combat, and reflexes. Examples: Free Fire, Call of Duty Mobile.

🧠 Strategy Games

Require planning and thinking. Examples: Clash of Clans, Chess apps, Boom Beach.

🚗 Racing Games

Compete in races using bikes, cars, etc. Examples: Asphalt 9, Real Racing 3.

🕵️ Adventure Games

Explore, solve puzzles, and follow stories. Examples: Genshin Impact, Harry Potter games.

⚽ Sports Games

Play digital versions of real-world sports. Examples: FIFA Mobile, World Cricket Championship.

👥 Multiplayer Games

Play online with friends or strangers. Examples: Among Us, PUBG, Ludo King.

🎲 Casual / Board Games

Simple, time-pass games. Examples: Candy Crush, Ludo, Carrom Pool.
